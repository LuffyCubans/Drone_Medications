* Welcome to My App *

This projects allows you to manage a fleet of drones to make fast and efficient deliveries. With our drones, you will be able to deliver medicines in places that are difficult to access in a safe and timely manner. Our platform offers the following functionalities:

  • Drone registration: Register your drones on our platform to start using them in your operations.
  • Medicine loading: Load the necessary medicines on each drone before delivery.
  • Loaded content check: You can check which drugs are loaded on each drone before sending it to its destination.
  • Availability of drones for cargo: Check the updated availability of drones available to load new shipments.
  • Drone Battery Level: Check the current battery level of the selected drone before starting a delivery.
